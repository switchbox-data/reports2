## il_pgl_rate_case/

Working notes for the **ICC Docket 26-0065** (Peoples Gas Light & Coke Co. 2026 rate case) testimony-support workstream. Combines a curtain-raiser briefing on what PGL is asking for in the new docket with a corpus study of the 10 intervener exemplars from the prior consolidated PGL/North Shore case (Dkts. 23-0068/23-0069, in `reports/il_npa/sources/`) that the team is using as drafting models. Style-guide, format-convention, tone, required-content, and form-difference files are all derived from that 10-filing corpus. Use these when scoping, structuring, or drafting Illinois ICC intervener testimony for 26-0065.
